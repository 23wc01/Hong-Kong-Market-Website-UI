About Us

◎

Goal

Even Columbia's major supermarkets only dedicated a measly aisle or two to Asian stables like rice, soy sauce, ramen noodles, etc. Thus, the goal was established: To better supply Asian immigrants in Columbia, Missouri with a wider selection of Asian products and produce.

 🏷

Description

Hong Kong Market is a store that sells groceries from Asia.

Owners

Chu-Jen Chen and his brother, I Kuang Chen, bought Hong Kong Market in 2010
🛍

Beginnings

As an immigrant from Taiwan himself, Chu-Jen Chen understood that for foreign immigrants in the US, food represented a connection to life back in the homelands. To be able to cook a traditional cuisine would allow immigrants to create a slice of home right here in America.

 🛩

Did you Know?

ATO BERT, INC. is affectionately named after a player piece in Taiwanese Monopoly. Ato Bert was the farmer piece on the Monopoly board who overcame obstacles and achieved success through perseverance and hard work, a symbol that Hong Kong Market and ATO BERT, INC. seeks to embody even today.
